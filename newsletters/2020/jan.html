Cloudflare integration

Our Cloudflare worker integration greatly increases Safari identification rates.
Safari Intelligent Tracking Prevention (ITP) works very well to prevent tracking of users.
ITP was first introduced in 2017, when Safari 11 was released. The goal of ITP is to prevent cross-site tracking by placing limitations on cookies and other website data.
With our cloudflare worker integration you can fix this.
